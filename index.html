Hooray! There's a new Thimble!

We've been hard at work building the new Thimble and it's finally out of beta. You can read all about it in this blog post. Now that the new version is ready, we've shut down the old version—that's why you're seeing this page!

Your old projects aren't lost!

When you sign in to the new Thimble, click on Your Projects in the dropdown menu to find them. To make them available for other people to see, you'll have to publish them again.

Try it at thimble.mozilla.org
