Hooray! There's a new Thimble!

We've been hard at work building the new Thimble, and it's finally ready, so we're shutting down the old version. Check out our blog post to learn all about it.

Your old projects aren't lost!

When you sign in to the new Thimble, you'll find them in the Your Projects section in the dropdown menu. To make them available for other people to see, you'll have to publish them again.

Try it at thimble.mozilla.org
